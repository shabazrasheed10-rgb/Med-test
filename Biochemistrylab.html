2021-20225 questions
1. Write the aim and the principle of benedicts test.

Aim: distinguish between reducing and nonreducing sugar for example distinguish between Sucrose and Glucose.
Principle: Carbohydrates with free carbonyl group have the ability to reduce solution of various metallic ions like (Cu+2) in alkaline medium to form insoluble red copperous oxide (Cu2O).
2. Write the aim and the principle of biuret test.

Aim: distinguish between Proteins and Amino acids, for example distinguish between Albumin and Alanine.
Principle: This test is positive with molecules contain two peptide bonds. The end reaction of the biuret test is dependent upon the formation of copper potassium biuret compound.
3. Enumerate the types of Titration.

1. Acid-base titration
2. Oxidation-reduction titration or Redox titration
3. Precipitation titration
4. Complexometric titration
